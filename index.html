Game Instructions

Test your memory and focus by uncovering all matching card pairs. Each round begins with a randomized layout, so no two games are ever the same.

  • 🃏 Flip two cards per turn to reveal what's underneath.
  • 🎯 If the cards match, they’ll disappear from the board.
  • 🔄 If they don’t, they’ll flip back over.
  • 🔁 You can restart the game whenever you'd like to try again or improve your score.
